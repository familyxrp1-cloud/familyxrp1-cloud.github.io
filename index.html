FAQ

QUESTIONS,
ANSWERED.

Why $FAMILY?
Built for the people who stayed. The XRP community proved its loyalty through years of uncertainty. $FAMILY reflects that. Community first, always.
What's the dev hold?
50% of supply locked in escrow. No rugs, no sudden dumps. Designed for long-term commitment from day one.
How does the burn work?
25% unlocks automatically 24 hours after launch on First Ledger and is burned forever. The remaining 75% stays locked for a minimum of one full year. That's the floor, not the ceiling.
What about the NFT collection?
A Family NFT collection is in the works as part of Phase 2. Details shared as it develops.
Is this just another meme coin?
$FAMILY is about community, not hype. No rocket emojis, no fake utility. Just people who believe in each other and the XRP ledger. Through every cycle.
